# Claims & constraints

## Allowed factual claims (grounded)
From product_echo_home.md:
- premium countertop / bartop arcade machine for home
- modern revival of classic ECHO bar-top arcade machines
- 149 built-in games
- plug and play
- no Wi-Fi required
- no downloads required
- B2C priority product
- product page: /en/echo
- home overview page: /en/home

## Forbidden / unsupported (do not claim)
- specific dimensions, weight, screen size, resolution
- specific game titles unless confirmed
- warranty length, return policy, shipping windows
- pricing or discounts
- "#1", "best", "leading" claims without sourced evidence
- comparisons against named competitors without sourced evidence
- health, safety, legal, or compliance statements
- environmental / sustainability claims
- statistics or "studies show" style claims without a verified source

## When information is missing
Write one of:
- `TODO: source not confirmed`
- `TODO: requires business confirmation`

## Editorial guardrails
- do not invent product facts
- do not force the product into irrelevant topics
- do not use sales-heavy or hype language (see brand_voice.md)
- product mentions must feel natural inside genuinely useful editorial content
