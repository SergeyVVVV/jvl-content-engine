# Internal links — rules

## Required links per article

- **1 mandatory link to `/en/echo`** — placed naturally, only where it genuinely fits the context
- **1–2 additional internal links** to other relevant pages:
  - homepage (`/en/home`)
  - other product pages
  - related blog posts
  - about-jvl
  - any other contextually relevant page

## Purpose

Strong internal linking connects informational blog content with commercial pages. It helps users navigate to relevant next steps and signals to Google which pages are priorities.

## Rules

- never force a link where it feels unnatural
- link placement should serve the reader, not just SEO
- do not repeat the same anchor text mechanically
- use descriptive, natural anchor text — not "click here" or "this page"
