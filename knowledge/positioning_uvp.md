# Positioning & UVP — JVL Echo Home

## One-line positioning
A premium, plug-and-play bartop arcade machine made for real adult homes - nostalgia made easy, built to last, and worth owning for years.

## Category
- home arcade / bartop arcade / touchscreen arcade for home
- adult home leisure product (not a gamer gadget, not a toy)

## Core UVP pillars (grounded in product_echo_home.md and brand_voice.md)
1. **Nostalgia, modernized** - a modern revival of the classic ECHO bar-top arcade format.
2. **149 built-in games** - a curated, ready-to-play library, no downloads.
3. **Plug and play** - no Wi-Fi required, no setup friction.
4. **Adult-home fit** - designed for home bars, basement lounges, dens, cabins, lake houses.
5. **Quietly premium** - made to feel tasteful, durable, and worth keeping.

## Who it is for
Mature, affluent homeowners (Mark & Linda Reynolds persona) seeking a meaningful, social, conversation-piece purchase for shared leisure spaces.

## Who it is NOT for
- Hardcore gamers, esports buyers, console upgraders
- Buyers chasing flashy, status-coded luxury
- Buyers wanting a kids' toy

- reviews / real customer proof
- quality and finish
- size / room fit
- warranty and support clarity
