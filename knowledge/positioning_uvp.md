# Positioning & UVP — JVL Echo Home

## One-line positioning

A premium, plug-and-play bartop arcade machine made for real adult homes — nostalgia made easy, built to last, and worth owning for years.

## Category anchors (use explicitly — image alone is not enough)

**Tier 1 — default:**
- home arcade
- bartop arcade
- touchscreen arcade for home
- arcade machine for home
- all-in-one arcade

**Tier 2 — support / use-case:**
- retro arcade for home
- 2-player arcade for home
- home bar arcade
- game room arcade
- arcade with built-in games

**Avoid as primary language:** console, gaming equipment, luxury, elite, cabinet, novelty gadget, countertop, tabletop

## Core UVP pillars

1. **Nostalgia, modernised** — a modern revival of the classic ECHO bartop arcade format
2. **149 built-in games** — a curated, ready-to-play library; no downloads; all JVL proprietary titles
3. **Plug and play** — no Wi-Fi required, no setup friction, works 100% offline
4. **Adult-home fit** — designed for home bars, basement lounges, dens, cabins, lake houses
5. **Quietly premium** — 22" touchscreen, 25W sound system, 360° swivel, built in the USA, 1-year warranty

## Message priority ladder (evidence-based, campaign-confirmed)

| Priority | Route | Role | Approved language zones |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1 | **Nostalgia** | Default first-touch route | bring back the classics, relive arcade nostalgia, old-school fun, childhood favourites, classic vibes |
| 2 | **Togetherness** | Best secondary route | across generations, game night, shared moments, friends over, family fun, everyone gathers here |
| 3 | **Ease / ownership** | Bridge from emotion to conversion | plug & play, set up in minutes, ready to play, just tap and play, no hassle |
| 4 | **Curated library value** | Rational support route | all the favourites in one place, instant classics library, built-in games — sell by gameplay style, not title name |
| 5 | **Adult-home fit / quiet premium** | Late-stage route only | made for real homes, looks right at home, built for grown-ups, designed in Canada made in the USA, 30 years of bartop craft |

**Campaign-confirmed rule (Easter Meta Ads, April 2026):** Emotion first, proof second. The text with the sharpest emotional hook followed by category + proof + CTA produced the lowest CPC ($0.18). Warranty/proof-first openers produced the worst CPC ($0.22). This is now an operating rule, not only a research recommendation.

## Core persuasion sequence

1. Open with nostalgia or togetherness
2. Clarify the category fast — home arcade, arcade machine for home, touchscreen arcade for home
3. Bridge to low-friction ownership — plug & play, all-in-one, easy to come back to
4. Close with proof — reviews, detail shots, room scale, warranty / return clarity, support, value justification

## Proof to lean on (confirmed claims)

- "Designed in Canada, made in the USA" — Tier 1 trust signal
- "Built on 30+ years of JVL bartop expertise. JVL bartops have been awarded 'Best Bartop Video Game of the Year' by RePlay Magazine multiple times." — heritage framing (do not attribute the award to ECHO directly)
- 22" HD touchscreen, 39.4 lbs, 15 × 19.5 × 18.5 inches
- 25-watt 4-speaker sound system with subwoofer
- 360° swivel base
- 1-year all-inclusive warranty; JVL covers shipping both ways
- 149 pre-installed proprietary games

## Who ECHO is for

Mature adult homeowners (The Grounded Nostalgia Buyer) seeking a meaningful, social, easy-to-own purchase for their home leisure spaces. See persona_echo_home.md for the full profile.

## Who ECHO is NOT for

- Hardcore gamers, esports buyers, console upgraders
- Buyers seeking flashy luxury or status-coded products
- Buyers shopping for children's toys

## Visual proof stack (priority order)

1. Demo video showing the machine working
2. Real-customer or credible in-use proof
3. Close-up of controls, screen, finish, materials, build quality
4. Scale-in-room proof — shows how it fits on real surfaces in real spaces
5. Adult-home room-setting imagery (den, basement lounge, home bar, cabin)
6. Origin / heritage proof asset ("Designed in Canada, made in the USA")

## Current strategic constraint

As of April 2026: ECHO has ~9 reviews on Amazon (4.3 average, only 3 verified with text or images). Until the review base is substantially stronger, every piece of content must carry a heavier-than-normal proof burden. Visual proof, written detail, warranty clarity, and origin/heritage signals must compensate for thin social proof.
