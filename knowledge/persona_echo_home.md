# Mark & Linda Reynolds — Echo Home persona

Audience summary:
Mark and Linda Reynolds represent mature, affluent homeowners who value nostalgia, quality, comfort, and meaningful home leisure.

Age:
- 55–72

Life stage:
- married or long-term couple
- adult or college-age children
- spacious home with dedicated leisure areas

Lifestyle:
- home bar
- basement lounge
- den
- private leisure spaces
- hosts friends and family
- values craftsmanship and lasting purchases
- prefers premium, meaningful objects over trendy gadgets

Core motivations:
- reconnect with arcade nostalgia
- create shared moments with spouse, family, and guests
- add a conversation-starting centerpiece to the home
- buy something with emotional value, not just entertainment value

What resonates:
- warm nostalgia
- premium but understated quality
- authenticity
- built to last
- social connection
- timeless memories
- easy plug-and-play enjoyment

Tone that fits:
- warm
- confident
- understatedly premium
- mature
- emotionally grounded
- never flashy or teenage-gamer style

Avoid:
- neon arcade clichés
- gamer slang
- tech-bro tone
- aggressive sales copy
- childish excitement
- "ultimate beast setup" type phrasing

Best environments:
- home bar
- basement recreation room
- whiskey lounge
- cabin
- lake house
- elegant home entertainment space
