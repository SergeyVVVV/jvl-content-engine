# Content directions — Echo Home B2C

Working list of content angles aligned to the Mark & Linda Reynolds persona and Echo Home product fit.

## Core editorial themes
1. **Home bar & basement lounge inspiration** - design ideas, layouts, lighting, ambience.
2. **Adult home leisure** - turning a den, basement, or cabin into a meaningful shared space.
3. **Nostalgia & shared moments** - the emotional value of reviving classic experiences.
4. **Game room buying guides** - how to choose, what to look for, what fits real adult homes.
5. **Hosting & entertaining at home** - guests, family, intergenerational moments.
6. **Bartop / home arcade buyer education** - format comparisons, setup, fit, ownership.

## Article types we use (mirrors brief_agent.md)
- informational
- informational_with_commercial_support
- commercial_investigation
- comparison
- buyer_guide
- lifestyle_inspiration

## Product integration rules
- product fit is **high** only when the topic naturally connects to home arcade ownership
- never force /en/echo into unrelated topics
- 1 mandatory natural link to /en/echo plus 1-2 relevant internal links (see internal_links.md)

## Out of scope for current MVP
- B2B / commercial venue content
- non-Echo product lines
