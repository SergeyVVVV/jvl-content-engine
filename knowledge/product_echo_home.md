# JVL Echo Home — product facts

## What is ECHO?

ECHO is a premium bartop arcade machine — once the king of bars and arcades — now re-engineered for the modern era. Compact, powerful, and packed with 149 games, it brings social gaming back to life in adult home environments.

**ECHO is the only 22" touchscreen bartop arcade on the market.**

## Product line

- **ECHO Home (B2C):** Free Play version — no bill validator, no coin acceptor. For residential use.
- **ECHO Commercial (B2B):** Includes bill acceptor ($1, $5, $10, $20; 500-bill capacity) and coin acceptor (quarters). For bars, restaurants, operators.

## Hardware features

- Super Bright 22" 16:9 HD LCD widescreen display
- 25-watt, 4-speaker high-fidelity tri-band sound system with a dedicated subwoofer
- 360° swivel base — players on both sides can take control; built for head-to-head play
- Dynamic multi-colour halo light around the screen with an ever-changing glow
- Reinforced plastic case and precision-built frame — built to withstand tough arcade and bar environments

- Adult-themed games can be turned on/off; availability schedule is configurable
- Customisation and system settings protected by secure key access
- Seven languages: English, Spanish, Italian, French, German, Polish, Russian
