# Keyword intent summary — Echo Home B2C

Use this as a working reference for SEO and brief agents. Intent matters more than volume (see seo_rules.md).

## Intent buckets

### Transactional / high product fit
- "bartop arcade machine for home"
- "home arcade machine with built-in games"
- "buy bartop arcade"
- "plug and play arcade machine"
- "where to buy arcade machines"

### Commercial investigation / mid product fit
- "best home arcade machine"
- "home arcade machine reviews"
- "bartop vs full-size arcade"
- "arcade machine for home bar"
- "2-player arcade for home"

### Informational / lower but supportable product fit
- "how to set up a home bar"
- "basement game room ideas"
- "gifts for retro arcade fans"
- "how to choose a home arcade machine"
- "home leisure room ideas for adults"

### Avoid (low intent / wrong audience)
- gamer-culture queries (esports, console, gaming rigs)
- kids arcade / kids party queries
- generic "arcade games online" queries

## Rules
- 1 primary keyword per article (must appear in title and slug)
- up to 10 secondary keywords used naturally
- prefer keywords closer to a real Echo Home buyer journey
