# ECHO premium touchscreen bartop arcade

## What is ECHO?

- ECHO is a premium bartop arcade machine - once the king of bars and arcades - now re-engineered for the modern era.
- Compact, powerful, and packed with 149 games, it brings social gaming back to life.
- ECHO is the only 22" touchscreen bartop arcade on the market.

## Product Modifications

- **Home:** Free Play Home version, without Bill Validator and Quarters Acceptor
- **Commercial:** everything from Home, plus:
  - Bill Acceptor (1$, 5$, 10$, 20$; capacity 500 bills)
  - Coin Acceptor (quarters)

## Product USPs

- Compact footprint, slick design, fits in just about anywhere - from cozy corners to game-ready bars.
- Touch controls - no mechanical failures, no lag. Every swipe, tap, and press delivers flawless, real-time response.
- 149 pre-installed games - no downloads, no installs required.
- No installation or setup needed - pure plug-n-play simplicity.
- Designed in Canada, made in the USA.
